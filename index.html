About Me

I’m a PhD student in Computational Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Georgia Institute of Technology. Before starting my PhD, I have been a postgrad student at Imperial College London where I earned my second Master’s degree in Computer Science.

As a PhD student, my research interests mainly include, but not limited to Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition and Cognitive Computing, and Deep Learning. In addition to my interests in pure computer science topics, I have also interested in application of such topics in down-to-earth engineering applications.

I am also working as a software developer (full stack web developer, iOS app and computational packages) and enjoying freelance consulting jobs.
